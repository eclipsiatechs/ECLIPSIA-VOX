Operational Guidelines: ECLIPSIA-VOX Platform

  • Platform Access: If the primary interface is concealed, please initiate access by clicking "Join ECLIPSIA-TECHS Channel" (links to our private Telegram) and subsequently selecting "Get Access" to validate entry.
  • Content Synthesis: Input your source text into the field. Select a designated Voice Personality from the menu, and execute the process by clicking the "Synthesize & Read Aloud" command.
  • Media Retrieval: Upon successful synthesis, the audio playback controls will become available. The audio file can be reviewed directly or downloaded as a WAV file for offline integration.

Service Interruption Protocol (429 Error)

  • Rate Limiting Notification: A "Limit reached, try again later" error indicates that the assigned API quota has been temporarily exceeded (HTTP 429 status).
  • Mandatory Action: A minimum delay of **60 seconds** is strictly required before reattempting synthesis. Repeated, rapid requests will maintain the service block.
